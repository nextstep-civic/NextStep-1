    Frequently Asked Questions

    Find quick answers to common questions

    Getting started is easy! Simply create an account on our platform, browse the events page to find opportunities that interest you, and sign up. You can also use our Compass chatbot to get personalized recommendations based on your interests and availability.

    NextStep organizes a wide variety of civic engagement events including community cleanups, town halls, voter registration drives, educational workshops, neighborhood meetings, and social impact projects. Check our Events page to see what's coming up!

    Badges are earned by participating in events and completing civic activities. Each event you attend contributes to your badge progress. Visit the Badges page to see all available badges and track your progress toward earning them.

    Absolutely! We're always looking for community partners who share our mission of promoting civic engagement. Use the contact form and select "Partnership Inquiry" to tell us about your organization and how we might work together.

    While we currently focus on the Boca Raton area, we welcome anyone who wants to contribute to civic engagement in our community. If you're interested in bringing NextStep to your area, please contact us!

    There are several ways to stay in the loop: follow us on social media, subscribe to our newsletter (form in the footer), check the Events page regularly, or enable notifications in your account settings to get alerts about new opportunities.
